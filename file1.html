Dear Ex,

As I reminisce our past,
I always thought,
We were having a blast.
But I guess not.

As I contemplate our fight,
I always surrender.
Because I don’t care who’s right,
I just want us to be together.

Now that it’s over,
I always wish I could’ve done better.
Be my real self this time.
But all I can do now is make this poem rhyme.

Not being able to talk to you,
Is so suffocating.
All I see is black and no hue,
And thats not a fun thing.

I mean, this is nothing new.
But all I wanted was to be with you.
Now that its not possible,
I guess this pain is acceptable.

As I think about us,
I always end up going to our chatbox.
And see those words that hit me like a bus.
The feeling just sucks.
